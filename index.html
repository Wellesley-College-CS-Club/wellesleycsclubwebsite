About

Wellesley CS Club is established to provide a forum for events within the CS major/minor community and to provide opportunities for enrichment through tech-related events
WORKSHOPS · Explore industry related workshops/job opportunities
SOCIAL EVENTS · Connect with other CS/MAS students and professors to build a stronger community among ourselves
WHACK · Build projects, have fun, be creative, break, learn, and ask questions in a space that celebrates diversity
